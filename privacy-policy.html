Privacy Policy

After Dark - Drinking Game

Effective date: March 27, 2026. This policy explains what information the app handles, how that information is used, and the choices available to users.

1. Summary

The app is designed to run locally on your device.

  • We do not require account creation.
  • We do not collect personal data on our servers.
  • We do not transmit player names, game session content, or gameplay choices to a remote server.
  • We do not request sensitive Android permissions such as location, contacts, camera, or microphone for core gameplay.

2. Information the App Handles

The app may handle the following data locally on your device:

  • Player-entered names used to display participants during a game session.
  • Game setup selections, such as vibe, intensity, deck choice, and enabled game options.
  • Local app state related to access tier, such as whether premium mode is enabled on the device.

This information is used only to operate the gameplay experience on the device.

3. Data Storage

The app uses local device storage for limited functionality.

  • A small amount of app state may be stored locally using on-device preferences.
  • Gameplay content, card decks, sound assets, and interface resources are bundled with the app.
  • Player data entered during gameplay is intended for local in-session use only.

We do not operate a backend system to receive or store this information remotely.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for advertising or marketing.

Because the app currently works locally and does not send gameplay data to our servers, we do not share player names or session details with external parties as part of normal app operation.

5. Analytics, Advertising, and Tracking

At the effective date of this policy:

  • The app does not include third-party advertising SDKs.
  • The app does not use analytics or behavioral tracking services to profile users.
  • The app does not use remote crash reporting services.

If a future version adds analytics, advertising, account systems, cloud sync, or other remote services, this Privacy Policy should be updated before those features are used in production.

6. Permissions

The app is not designed to require sensitive permissions for normal gameplay. If platform-level access is introduced in a future release, the app listing and this Privacy Policy should be updated accordingly.

7. Children's Privacy

This app is a drinking game and may include mature, suggestive, or alcohol-related prompts. It is not intended for children.

If your local laws require a minimum age for alcohol-related content or drinking activities, you should not use the app unless you meet that age requirement.

8. Safety and Responsible Use

The app is intended for entertainment only. Users are responsible for complying with local laws and acting responsibly.

  • Do not use the app while driving or in unsafe situations.
  • Do not participate in drinking activities if you are under the legal drinking age in your location.
  • Non-alcoholic alternatives may be used at any time.

9. Your Choices

Because the app primarily stores limited information locally on your device, you can generally remove locally stored app data by uninstalling the app or clearing the app's storage through your device settings.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date above and publish the revised policy where the app is distributed or hosted.
